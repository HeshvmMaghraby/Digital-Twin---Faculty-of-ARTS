Chapter One: Introduction

Project Vision

The vision is to architect a comprehensive Digital Twin framework for Cairo University campus, focusing on the Languages & Translation Building and the Main Faculty of Arts Building. It aims to establish an immersive digital environment mirroring physical infrastructure in spatial and operational dimensions.

Problem Statement

Educational facilities face challenges categorized into Administrative (inefficient resource allocation, lack of real-time oversight) and Technical dimensions (absence of smart monitoring for energy and occupancy).

Project Objectives

  • Create accurate digital representation of spatial characteristics.
  • Simulate daily activities and user movement.
  • Analyze space utilization efficiency.
  • Conduct comparative analysis between buildings.
  • Monitor energy and resource consumption.
  • Support data-driven decision making.
  • Improve operational efficiency.
  • Develop a scalable framework for other buildings.
