Digital Twin
Faculty of Arts - Cairo University

The future of smart spatial planning. An interactive digital model integrating spatial and engineering data to create an exact replica of the campus, facilitating facility management and highly accurate spatial data analysis.

Explore 3D Model Interactive Maps

Project Phases

📐

1. Spatial Data Acquisition

Utilizing advanced surveying equipment (Total Station, Laser Scanners) and Photogrammetry to accurately capture the exact dimensions of the Faculty of Arts buildings.

🏛️

2. 3D Modeling (BIM)

Transforming the raw spatial data into highly detailed 3D architectural models that represent the structural reality of the campus.

🔗

3. Data Integration & Twin

Linking the 3D models with descriptive and functional data (GIS integration) to create a live, interactive Digital Twin for smart management.
